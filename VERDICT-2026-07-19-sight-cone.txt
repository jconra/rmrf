VERDICT — 2026-07-19 sight cone (stealth phase, slice 1: the cone itself)
========================================================================

Units no longer see in a 360° circle. Visual ENEMY detection now falls off by how far the
target sits off the unit's LOOK direction: full range in the forward ±45° arc, tapering to
HALF at the flanks (90°), to BLIND behind (135°+). Gradual falloff (no hard step). A turreted
unit aims the cone by its TURRET (Lurcher reads the live mount angle — idle-sweeps ±34° when
parked so it scans, snaps onto a target when it engages; Jotun too, tiny arc); Firebrat/
Valkyrie have no turret → hull heading. Sound stays a full 360° (soundSources unchanged), so
a rear flanker is still HEARD and the unit turns to investigate. Supply/scrap discovery stays
360° this slice — the one changed variable is combat visual detection. Behind RR.setSightCone
(?nocone) + RR.setConeAngles.

THIS IS A NERF, BY DESIGN (Jacob: "it makes the game more interesting"). The gate is a
GUARDRAIL check — does the game stay healthy — NOT a win-rate scoreboard. A weaker-but-more-
interesting AI is the goal (see feedback_smarter_over_winrate).

GATE (same build, cone ON default vs cone OFF=?nocone — byte-identical but the one flag, so
NO cross-build chaos shuffle; 30 deterministic seeds @1500s):

THE ALARM DROP IS REAL: a 360° circle makes an edge target blink in/out of detection from any
bearing, and that boundary flicker feeds the engage/KITE strobe we hunted on 07-18. A cone
gives stable "what's in front" detection, so there's LESS strobe. The nerf calmed the thing we
were chasing.

THE STUCK INCREASE IS A MIRAGE (one unit, one map): seed 137 red firebrat wedges a shore
corner @(59,131) on the capture step — 92s OFF, 784s ON. That single episode is ~346 of the
~245-sample total increase, i.e. EVERY OTHER SEED COMBINED got LESS stuck with the cone on.
Seed 137 is stale in BOTH builds (nobody wins either way); same shore-wedge nav pathology as
seed 95, just left to sit longer because no enemy wandered over to dislodge it. Pre-existing
"long-GOTO / shore-wedge" residual, NOT a cone regression.

VERDICT: KEEP as default. Guardrails hold (resolution up, no new never-finishers), the game
gets the flankable stealth Jacob wants, and it quietly REDUCED combat flap. The +13% resolve
time is the fair price of half-blind units.
