TOWER UPGRADES — verdict (2026-07-11)
=====================================

FEATURE (Jacob's design): a single linear STAR path per corner tower, bought from the
garage scrap shop. Four colour bands x 3 stars = 12:
  bronze 1-3 -> +HP   silver 4-6 -> +range   gold 7-9 -> +fire rate   black 10-12 -> +damage
Per star: +20% HP / +12% range / -11% cd / +18% dmg (within its band). A maxed tower goes
340->544 HP, range 54->73, cd 1.6->1.07, dmg 18->28. Reuses the promotion star billboard
(no new tower models). Player flow: scrap shop -> outside aerial picker -> pick a tower, read
its stats/stars -> a jeep rolls out and installs the star (~5s; killable the whole time).
Treasure box: a destroyed upgraded tower spills scrap (~half the stars, as loose piles).
Partial rebuild: a rebuilt tower keeps a random 40-75% of its old stars.

AI: orderRepair gained a 3rd priority (after repair-wounded-gun, after buy-replacement-gun):
aiWantsUpgrade (bank >= AI_UPG_SCRAP 6, fleet >= 4) -> findUpgradeTower (least-upgraded HEALTHY
standing tower, spreads stars across the ring) -> deployUpgrade. Knob RR.setAiUpgrades.

ECONOMY NOTE: teams bank little scrap (vehicle rebuilds are the first claim). At the first
threshold (14) the AI never upgraded; dropped to 6, where upgrades fire in ~5/8 seeds
(14 stars / 8 sample matches; one rich seed fortified to 9 stars).

A/B TOURNAMENT (30 seeds, rng-paired, RR.setAiUpgrades off vs on)
                      OFF (baseline)   ON
  resolved            26/30            26/30
  avg resolve         556s             555s
  stalemates          4                4     (2 seeds shared, 2 shifted — no systemic block)
  transit-stuck       1214             1248  (noise)
  idle-at-goal        1057             847   (-20%)
  HP healed/match     748              770
  guns bought/mounted 15/10            9/5

READ: AI tower upgrades are RESOLUTION-NEUTRAL — same win rate, same time, same stalemate
count (different seeds). Tougher AI towers do NOT choke out attackers into stalemates, which
was the main risk. Side effect: the AI buys FEWER replacement guns (15->9) because small
upgrade spends leak scrap below the 10-scrap gun threshold — it fortifies live towers with
stars instead of re-gunning dead ones. That's a wash for outcomes; if it ever reads as the
AI neglecting dead guns, raise AI_UPG_SCRAP toward/above AI_GUN_SCRAP (10) so guns fund first.

VERDICT: keep it (per smarter-over-winrate — the AI now uses the feature, at no cost to
resolution). Threshold 6 + the gun/upgrade split are tuning knobs for Jacob's playtest.
